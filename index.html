When Do You Need Sports Psychotherapy?

  To enhance performance:

You know you have potential for excellence and you want to learn how your mind can help you achieve it.

You’ve heard of "The Zone" and you want to learn how to go there.

  To overcome mental obstacles:

You keep taking lessons and you just don’t seem to improve your game.

You worry a lot and lose confidence.

You feel sick, bored or angry about playing.

Your coach says you need to work on your attitude.

 

Our clients reach their goals quickly, utilizing solutions based on their unique needs.

About the Sports Psychotherapy Center:

We counsel teenagers and adults who participate in sports and want to achieve peak performance.

We identify how your mind influences your body.

Together we create strategies to build and strengthen a positive mental attitude.

You learn to overcome mental obstacles that interfere with your body doing what it already knows how to do.
