The pamphlet  Stop That Racket - Quiet Your Mind for Winning Tennis, written by Sylvia Feldman and Sheila Rowny, contains the wisdom of decades of teaching tennis, and is geared towards the recreational competitive tennis player.

Syl Feldman is a USPTA USPTR PRO-1 who has been teaching tennis for more than thirty years. Her fun and passion has always been, and continues to be, combining “inner and outer” tennis skills for success in tennis teaching.

Sheila Rowny is a psychotherapist with over twenty years of experience, and a special interest in sports performance enhancement. She is also an accomplished tennis player, who continually strives to improve her own “inner game”.
